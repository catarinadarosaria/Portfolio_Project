MACAM

Contemporary Art Museum & Hotel

MACAM - Armando Martins Museum of Contemporary Art is a new cultural project dedicated to modern and contemporary art, which will open in Lisbon in the first half of 2024. It is an unprecedented initiative that, for the first time in Portugal and Europe, integrates a 5-star hotel into the space of a museum.
