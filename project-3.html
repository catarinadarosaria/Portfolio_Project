Birra Gallery

Visual Identity & Social Media Design for an Art Gallery

Birra is a space/gallery/workshop that aims to create a community of artists from all backgrounds and domains. It serves as a creative and exhibition space. Within the Birra space, you can find works ranging from ceramics to painting, photography, and independent publishing. This space also promotes various activities within its premises, such as workshops, conversations, exhibitions, and art fairs.
