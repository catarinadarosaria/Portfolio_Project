Taxi Driver

From screen light into paper matter: Transformation of the movie “Taxi Driver” by Martin Scorsese into a book, through its script

This project aims to transform the movie "Taxi driver" into a book. It's an experimental work that seeks to break the barrier between these two ways of communication. It is a transcription of something that emerges from the screen light into paper format. Throughout this editorial project, the reader is intended to feel the anxieties and tensions of the movie through its graphic approach.
