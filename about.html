Biography

Catarina da Rosária, a 27-year-old multidisciplinary designer originally from Porto, Portugal, but now residing in Lille, France, explores a diverse range of artistic disciplines in her work. Her creative journey encompasses graphic design, photography, sculpture, and painting. Recently, she has developed a keen interest in web development and embarked on a learning journey through the SheCodes Max Workshop. There, she gained proficiency in JavaScript, React, and responsive design.
