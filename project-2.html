Distinct.e.s Collectif

Visual Identity & Social Media Design for a Queer Collective

Distinct.e.s is a queer collective based in Lille, France, focused on organizing diverse events throughout the city aimed at showcasing queer artists. From poetry slams and art fairs to performances and music festivals, the collective aims to provide visibility to the queer community. The visual identity developed for this collective is designed to be vibrant, dynamic, and reflect the versatility of the various events it organizes.
